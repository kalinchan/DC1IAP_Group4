Our Services

Drone Drop offers a broad range of services that encompass everything your organisation will need to start implementing sustainable, affordable, long-term delivery solutions using drone-based technology. Drone Drop will take you through the whole process from designing infrastructure to managing phased implementation. All of Drone Drops services are completely bespoke, so you can be sure your organisation's exact needs are met.

Technology

Drone Drop will source all the drone technology you will need for the requirements you specify from our official partners WeMakeDrones INC, TransportDrones LTD and GPUyou. We'll manage cost, transport of drones, customisation of hardware and all things in-between. Drone Drop also offers software development services, so you can leave all the software needed to execute your drone-based infrastructure plans to us. Our highly skilled, specialist engineers will always be on hand to update and maintain software as per your requirement.

Consultancy

If you want to take your organisation to the next level with drones but aren't sure how, we offer consultancy to walk you through the whole process. If you choose to work with us, we'll make sure you're ahead of your delivery competitors with professionally implemented solutions thanks to advice from our drone delivery experts and researchers. We'll even design your infrastructure based on a design brief from the ground up.

Management

We will work in partnership with your organisation, offering long-term management services to maintain your new drone-based delivery systems into the future. As part of our management offerings, we will keep software up-to-date with new features and improvements; train staff to use, repair or develop for drone technology and much more. Get in touch with us through our contact page to see what else we can offer for your organisation.
